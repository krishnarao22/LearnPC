Introduction to Hardware

What's inside our devices?

Hardware is the physical side to our devices. For example, keyboards, mice, and monitors are all hardware.

A good rule of thumb to remember:

Anything you can (or could) touch phyically is hardware!

Hardware is just one of the main elements of a computer. The other is software.
